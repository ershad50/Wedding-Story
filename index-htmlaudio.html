The Story

It start in this beautiful New York City. We met in a small restaurant and keep contact from that night. A happy chatting always with us every day and every night. One day we decided to met again at that restaurant. It was our first date, a date that we will never forget it, a date that freeze everything, a very nervous date, with a full of happiness.

Our new story start from here, when we had a trip to the beach. A nice beach with a warm sand, blue sea and a nice sky. We enjoy our time together, wait for sunrise and sunset. And then a romantic wedding proposal fill this moment. A happy smile, hug, kiss, beautiful sky, the orange sunrise and sound of the waves complete this great moment.

And today... We are Getting Married
